About us

We are an environmentalist group that research about the ethical impacts technology has on the world and have made this website dedicated to our research.

We hope to enlighten people with a better understanding and the reality of the impact that technology has in this world.

As we know technology has developed exponentially in the past few decades and who knows when there will be the limit.

However as you may know within the development there has been quite a lot of disturbance nature and that is what we , as a community, will be presenting.
